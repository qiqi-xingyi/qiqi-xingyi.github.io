About

I build hardware-aware quantum workflows for protein active-site structure prediction and docking. My work emphasizes real quantum processors (IBM Quantum) with classical post-processing for structure reconstruction and evaluation (e.g., RMSD, docking consistency).

  • Keywords: VQE, Quantum Sampling, Protein Active Sites
  • Interests: noise-robust sampling, energy-guided clustering, docking evaluation

Research Overview

Real-Processor Quantum Workflows

End-to-end pipelines that encode amino-acid sequences, execute on utility-level quantum hardware, and reconstruct 3D active-site conformations under physical constraints.

Sampling-Driven Reconstruction

Quantum sampling for diverse conformations, followed by classical clustering and energy-guided refinement to recover stable structures and docking-consistent poses.

Evaluation & Benchmarks

Docking tests, RMSD analysis, and curated benchmark datasets to assess practicality and robustness of quantum pipelines on real devices.
