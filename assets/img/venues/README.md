# Venue marks

- `sc25-light.png` and `sc25-dark.png` are the official transparent horizontal SC25 logos downloaded from the SC25 [Photos & Logos](https://sc25.supercomputing.org/media/photos-logos/) page. The black/white text variants are selected to match the site theme.
- `sc26-light.png` and `sc26-dark.png` are the official transparent horizontal SC26 logos downloaded from the SC26 [Photos & Logos](https://sc26.supercomputing.org/media/photos-logos/) page. The black/white text variants are selected to match the site theme.
- The older `sc25.svg` and `sc26.svg` header marks are retained for provenance but are no longer used by the publication list.
- Advanced Science is represented by a small typographic venue label in the page markup rather than a copied image asset.
